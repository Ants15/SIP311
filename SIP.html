Project Description:

This project aims to create a new version of AI Chat Bot that will connect to individuals on a website and create an avatar based off of the consumer’s profile. Currently I do not have any team members, and I aim to complete the portion of the MVP for SIP311 solo. I will be mapping out the blueprint with wire frames and generating the full idea behind what I would like the app to look like. Then, I will build the ideas behind the functionality and how the AI will move around on websites to help the customer base. I will also be choosing the best software for the type of app that is based on research into AI apps and data. All the steps work towards a finalized idea and scope behind the idea. I will also be using Trello’s free version to monitor and record all the steps to get from point ‘a’ to ‘b’.

Innovation Claim:

AI Buddy will allow AI generated messages to come across as a personalized AI 3D face that is customer based. Aiming to improve the AI that is being utilized across a multitude of websites today. Innovation: Chat Bot with a 3D avatar being able to be utilized through many different websites. Innovative MVP: Prototype through Protopie, wireframes of how the AI bot will work, and SEO and the CTA.
